Misinformation Research Study

Welcome to our research study!

This study examines how people process and evaluate information they encounter online. Your participation will help us understand how different communication strategies affect belief formation.

What you'll do:

  • Provide basic demographic information
  • Read and evaluate an information statement
  • Engage in a brief discussion about the topic
  • Provide a final evaluation

Important notes:

  • Takes approximately 5-10 minutes
  • Your responses are anonymous
  • Data is used for research purposes only
  • You can exit at any time

By continuing, you consent to participate in this research study.
